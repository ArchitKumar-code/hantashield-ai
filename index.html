Hantavirus Information Center

Symptoms +
Hantavirus symptoms include fever, muscle pain, fatigue, chills, headaches, nausea and breathing difficulty in severe stages. Early medical attention can improve survival outcomes.
Mode of Transmission +
Hantavirus spreads through exposure to infected rodent urine, saliva and droppings. Humans may inhale contaminated airborne particles.
Environmental Risk Factors +
High humidity, heavy rainfall, dense vegetation and flood-prone regions may increase rodent activity and environmental vulnerability.
About HantaShield AI +
HantaShield AI combines environmental analytics, climate intelligence and epidemiological reasoning to estimate hantavirus vulnerability levels.
